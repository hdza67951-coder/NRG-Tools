📋 VERBATIM SCRIPT — Fill in blanks before reading

In order to ensure the enrollment is processed correctly, I just need to confirm the following. I am speaking with ___________ and you are authorized to incur charges for and are the decision maker for the property at __________________ and would like to enroll in: _________________

The provider of the plan is NRG Protects Inc.

The cost of the plan(s) you purchased is _________ for a 12-month term. Coverage under your plan will begin on the plan start date shown on your coverage letter, subject to any applicable waiting period; and, if this plan is set for auto-renewal, coverage will continue for the initial term and each renewal term, at the then-current renewal price, and will end with either of the following, whichever occurs first:

  • (a) failure to receive your plan payment in accordance with the terms and conditions of the plan; or
  • (b) coverage under the plan is cancelled by you or NRG Protects in accordance with the terms and conditions of the plan.

The plan must continue without any lapse in payment during the contract period for you to receive coverage.

This plan may be cancelled at any time within 30 days of your plan start date and you will receive a full refund if no services were performed prior to cancellation. All other cancellation and refund requests will be processed according to our terms and conditions.

Other exclusions and terms and conditions apply. You will receive a copy of your terms and conditions.

To file a claim, ask any questions regarding your recurring payment for your plan(s), or revoke or change your authorization for the recurring payment, please contact NRG Protects at 855-334-3577.

The terms and conditions of the plan may limit NRG Protects' contract obligations due to coverage caps, coverage exclusions, and cash back option in the plan documents, so please carefully review your contract when it is received.

You may also visit www.nrgprotects.com for more information.

Common Objections After Reading Terms

❌ "I can't afford it / too expensive"

"Ok Sir, I understand, but remember that it's better to have a protection plan than not to use it too much; instead of needing services and not having them, you take more money out of pocket. And I think $10.99 is not much — just think how much tech parts and labor cost."

❌ "I don't want autopay"

"OK, I understand you don't like autopay, but you have worked with us for a long time. You know we don't play with your money, and autopay is better because sometimes banks take longer to send payments to us. I don't want you to wait for a tech because of a delay in your payment."

❌ "I don't need it / don't see the benefit"

"Yes, you don't need it now unless you have home insurance. You might not need it now, but depending on the age of your home, things get older and they need to be fixed — and it's only $10.99."

❌ "I need to talk to someone else first"

"Remember, I will send all the documents so you can read and share with your family. It's better if they can read it too — but if you don't like what you see in the letter, call me back and I'll clear any other questions."

Objeciones Comunes al Leer los Términos

❌ "No me alcanza / está caro"

"Ok señor/a, entiendo, pero recuerde que es mejor tener un plan de protección que no tenerlo; en vez de necesitar el servicio y no tenerlo, y sacar más dinero de su bolsillo. Y creo que $10.99 no es mucho — piense cuánto cuestan las piezas técnicas y la mano de obra."

❌ "No quiero autopago"

"OK, entiendo que no le gusta el autopago, pero usted lleva mucho tiempo trabajando con nosotros. Sabe que no jugamos con su dinero, y el autopago es mejor porque a veces los bancos tardan más en enviarnos los pagos. No quiero que tenga que esperar al técnico por un retraso en su pago."

❌ "No lo necesito / no veo el beneficio"

"Sí, quizás no lo necesita ahora, a menos que ya tenga un seguro de hogar. Puede que no lo necesite ahora, pero dependiendo de la edad de su casa, las cosas se vuelven más viejas y necesitan reparación — y solo son $10.99."

❌ "Necesito hablar con alguien más"

"Recuerde que le enviaré todos los documentos para que pueda leerlos y compartirlos con su familia. Es mejor que ellos también puedan leerlo — pero si no le gusta lo que ve en la carta, llámeme y aclaro cualquier otra pregunta."
